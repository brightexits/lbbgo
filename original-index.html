Do you work with brand-new stores or only established ones? +
We specialize in helping Shopify stores that already have products and are live, even if you’ve only made a handful of sales. If you’re just starting, we’ll point you in the right direction.
What exactly do you manage for me? +
We handle paid ads, creative production, email marketing, CRO landing pages, and AI-powered operations. You focus on your product and brand while we scale traffic and sales.
How soon can I expect results? +
Most clients start seeing meaningful improvements within the first 30 days. We focus on quick wins while building long-term growth systems.
Do I keep control of my accounts? +
Yes. Your Shopify, ad accounts, and email platforms remain under your ownership. We plug in our systems and expertise.

No. Our fee covers strategy, management, creative, CRO, and reporting. Ad spend is paid directly to platforms like Meta and Google and stays fully under your control.
What if I’ve tried ads before and failed? +
That’s where we come in. We audit and fix creative, targeting, and funnel issues, then rebuild for profitable acquisition.
Is this a course or coaching program? +
No. We’re not a course. We execute for you, with transparent reporting and strategic guidance along the way.
What’s included in your fee? +
Ad management, creative production, landing pages and CRO, email flows, reporting, and ongoing support. No hidden costs.
How long are your contracts? +
No. We offer flexible options, from one-time packages to ongoing retainers , based on your goals. You’re free to stop at any time.
Why choose Launch Big Brands instead of freelancers? +
Freelancers cover single pieces. We give you an integrated senior team running every growth lever together.
